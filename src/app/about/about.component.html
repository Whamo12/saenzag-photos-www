Alejandro Saenz

Biography

Alejandro is a freelance Photographer and an Application Security Consultant based in Reston, Virginia. He received his Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from VCU in 2013 and he has been working in the IT field ever since. When he is not coding or hacking away, Alejandro enjoys adventuring through life with his camera by his side.
